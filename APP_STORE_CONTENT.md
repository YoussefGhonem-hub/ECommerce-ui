## Privacy Policy (Key Points to Include)

Your privacy policy should cover:

1. **Information Collection**

   - Account information (name, email, phone)
   - Shipping addresses
   - Payment information (note: not stored, only processed)
   - Browsing behavior
   - Device information
   - Location data (if used)

2. **How We Use Information**

   - Process orders and payments
   - Communicate about orders
   - Improve app experience
   - Send promotional offers (with consent)
   - Prevent fraud

3. **Information Sharing**

   - Payment processors
   - Delivery partners
   - Analytics providers
   - Never sold to third parties

4. **Data Security**

   - Encryption methods
   - Secure servers
   - Regular security audits

5. **User Rights**

   - Access your data
   - Request deletion
   - Opt-out of marketing
   - Update information

6. **Contact Information**
   - Email for privacy concerns
   - Data protection officer (if applicable)

## Tips for Better App Store Optimization (ASO)

1. **Use relevant keywords** in your title and description
2. **Highlight unique features** early in the description
3. **Use bullet points** for easy scanning
4. **Include social proof** (reviews, ratings) when available
5. **Update regularly** with new content and features
6. **Respond to reviews** to show active development
7. **Use high-quality screenshots** showing key features
8. **Create a compelling feature graphic** for Play Store
9. **Localize content** for different markets if targeting multiple countries
10. **A/B test** different descriptions and screenshots
